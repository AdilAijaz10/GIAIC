About Us

The main goal of our AR-Based computer fundamentals App is to help students visualize hardware components and devices of computer in 3D using AR technology. These hardware devices and components are present in the computer fundamentals course that students study in their first semester of BS Computer Engineering. Nowadays, traditional education methods lack interactivity and engagement with students. This lack of interaction decrease students interest in the Computer Fundamentals subject. Apart from this, most educational institutions don't offer physical access to the computer components and devices. Due to which, students are not able to get practical experience of the computer components and devices. Our AR-CF app solves all these issues by providing students with 3d visualizations of computer components and devices in their mobile phones. Students can also interact with these 3D models using fingers gestures. They can rotate and zoom the 3d models any way they want. With AR-CF application, they don't need physical access to these costly devices and components, so our solution is also very cost effective while also providing students with free access to internal components of computer.
